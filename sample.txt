# Here are 10 quotes about success:

1. "Success is not final, failure is not fatal: It is the courage to continue that counts." # Winston Churchill

2. "Success usually comes to those who are too busy to be looking for it." # Henry David Thoreau

3. "Don't be afraid to give up the good to go for the great." # John D. Rockefeller

4. "I find that the harder I work, the more luck I seem to have." # Thomas Jefferson

5. "Success is not in what you have, but who you are." # Bo Bennett

6. "The only place where success comes before work is in the dictionary." # Vidal Sassoon

7. "Success is walking from failure to failure with no loss of enthusiasm." # Winston Churchill

8. "The way to get started is to quit talking and begin doing." # Walt Disney

9. "Success is how high you bounce when you hit bottom." # George S. Patton

10. "Success is a journey, not a destination. The doing is often more important than the outcome." # Arthur Ashe
